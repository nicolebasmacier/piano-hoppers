# Problem
I want tiny jumping robots to play my piano. It doesn't exist, so it must. A future idea for this project, if successful, would be for the robots to play a 4 hands piano piece with me. 

# Project Scope

## Phase 1: Single Robot

Build a single robot that starts on a piano tile and jumps from tile to tile to play a melody. 


## Phase 2: Robot Swarm

Build multiple jumping robots and place them all on the same piano. They should jump at varying heights to play a more complex piece. 

## Phase 3: Expressive Performance

The design must support:

- Dynamics:
Piano piano (very soft), piano (soft), mezzo-piano (medium soft), mezzo-forte (medium loud), forte (loud), forte forte (very loud).

- Articulation:
Staccato, legato, and crescendo/decrescendo

- Tempo:
A range of tempos as well as ritardando/accelerando (slowing down and speeding up) 

- Noise:
The ideal performnace shouldn't have tapping sounds every time the robots land on a key. The robots should also run quieter than the piece they're playing.
